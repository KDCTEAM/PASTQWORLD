how to check NECO result for 2023 june/july SSCE

prerequsite for accessing 2023 june/jule SSCE result

acqiring the neco token(scratch card) is a fundamental need and a most. without the token, one can not gain access to his/her own result.


a step-by-step guide

(1.)begin by visiting the NECO result portal: https://results.neco.gov.ng/

(2.)determine your examination year: for this specifc year should be 2023

(3.)speify your examination format: select 'SSCE' internal (JUN/JUL)'

(4.)submit necessary details: carefully input your token

(5.)get result: get your result by clicking on 'check result'.
