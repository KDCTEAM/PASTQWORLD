JAMB Admission 2024: How to Accept or Reject Without Your SIM Card

If you've misplaced or damaged your JAMB SIM card, don't worry! JAMB has introduced an alternative solution for accepting or rejecting admission offers for the 2024 exercise. Instead of using the USSD codes, you can now visit a JAMB-approved center to manage your admission status.

Steps to Accept or Reject Admission

  1. Find a JAMB-approved center or accredited CBT center near you.
  2. Bring your JAMB registration slip and a valid ID card.
  3. Inform the center's staff that you need to accept or reject your admission.

To Accept Admission:

  • Provide your JAMB details and confirm your admission status.
  • The staff will accept the admission on your behalf.
  • Ensure you receive a printed confirmation.

To Reject Admission:

  • Clearly state your intention to reject the offer.
  • Verify your details, including your registration number and ID.
  • The staff will handle the rejection process.
  • Obtain a printed document confirming the rejection.

Remember to:

  • Check center availability before visiting.
  • Prepare for possible fees.
  • Keep all documents provided by the center for future reference.
