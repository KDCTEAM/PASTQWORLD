1

A second alkanol can be oxidized to give an

A

alkanone

B

alkyne

C

alkanoic acid

D

alkanal

Correct Option: A


Solution

With a suitable oxidising agent (such as acidified dichromate solution, or, acidified permanganate solution), secondary alkanols (alcohols) can be oxidised to alkanones (ketones).
